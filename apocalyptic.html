Natural Disaster Apocalypse

Movies depicting catastrophic events caused by natural forces, threatening humanity's survival.

2012 (2009)

Overview

As Earth faces cataclysmic natural disasters triggered by solar flares, a man fights to save his family amidst global chaos.

Don't Look Up (2021)

Overview

Two astronomers struggle to warn humanity about an impending comet collision, satirizing societal apathy and media dysfunction.

Pandemic Apocalypse

Movies centered on deadly viruses or infections that decimate populations and threaten humanity, often exploring societal breakdown.

Contagion (2011)

Overview

A deadly virus spreads globally, triggering panic and a race to find a cure as society unravels.

I Am Legend (2007)

Overview

A lone scientist in a deserted New York City searches for a cure to a virus that turned humans into monstrous mutants.

World War Z (2013)

Overview

A former UN investigator travels the globe to stop a fast-spreading zombie pandemic threatening human extinction.

Societal Collapse and Rebuild

Stories of civilizations falling and the struggle to rebuild or survive in the aftermath.

Dawn of the Planet of the Apes (2014)

Overview

Tensions between intelligent apes and human survivors escalate into war, testing loyalties and survival.

Rise of the Planet of the Apes (2011)

Overview

A scientist’s experiment with a cognitive-enhancing drug leads to the rise of intelligent apes and humanity’s downfall.

War for the Planet of the Apes (2017)

Overview

Caesar leads the apes in a final stand against a ruthless human army, seeking peace amidst betrayal and loss.

Snowpiercer (2013)

Overview

Survivors of a frozen apocalypse live on a perpetually moving train, where class divisions spark a rebellion against the oppressive elite.

Twelve Monkeys (1995)

Overview

A prisoner is sent back in time to prevent a viral plague that destroyed the world.

Post-Apocalyptic Survival

Tales of individuals or groups navigating harsh, desolate worlds after catastrophic events.

A Quiet Place Part II (2020)

Overview

After surviving alien creatures that hunt by sound, a family ventures into a ravaged world, facing new threats and survivors.

Mad Max: Fury Road (2015)

Overview

In a desolate wasteland, Max teams up with Furiosa to escape a tyrannical warlord and his fanatical followers in a high-octane chase across a ravaged world.

Maze Runner: The Death Cure(2018)

Overview

The final chapter follows the group’s mission to rescue friends and stop a corrupt organization in a crumbling city.

Maze Runner: The Scorch Trials (2015)

Overview

After escaping the maze, the group faces a desolate wasteland and a powerful organization with sinister motives.

The Maze Runner (2014)

Overview

A group of teens trapped in a mysterious maze must uncover its secrets to escape, facing deadly creatures and hidden truths.

Scorched Earth (2018)

Overview

A bounty hunter tracks criminals in a post-apocalyptic world ravaged by environmental collapse, facing moral dilemmas along the way.

The Last Survivors (2014)

Overview

In a drought-ravaged world, a young woman defends her water source from scavengers while uncovering a sinister plot.

What Still Remains (2018)

Overview

A young woman joins a mysterious community in a post-apocalyptic world, navigating dangers and questioning their true intentions.
